Giraffe

According to website thoughtco.com, the giraffe is the tallest living animal, occasionally reaching heights of 40 feet!

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Average Height: 16-19 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years in the wild
  • Habitat: African savannas

One of the most recognizable animals in the world, giraffes, while all belonging to the same genus and species, can be differentiated into their nine subspecies based on the colors and patterns of their coat. When a female giraffe is giving birth to her newborn, the newborn often falls a distance of five feet as his first few moments outside of his mother's womb!
